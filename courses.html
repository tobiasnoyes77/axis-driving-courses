Frequently Asked Questions

Is this course right for me?

This course is for you if a judge or lawyer told you to go to driving school, if the DMV notified you to take a course because of too many demerit points, or if you want to qualify for an insurance discount.

How fast can I finish?

Virginia requires state-mandated timers on each page, so if you move straight through without stopping, the minimum time is 8 hours.

What's your refund policy?

We offer a full refund — no questions asked — as long as you request it before you finish the course.

When do I get my completion certificate?

Immediately after you finish. You'll receive your completion certificate right away as a downloadable PDF.

Do I have to complete it in one sitting?

No. You can log in and out as often as you need. Your progress is automatically saved, so you can pick up right where you left off.

What devices does it work on?

It works on all devices (phones, tablets, and computers) and in all major web browsers.

What happens if I fail a quiz or knowledge check?

You can retake quizzes and knowledge checks as many times as you need. To date, 99% of students pass, and if you still can't get through it, we'll give you a complete refund.
