Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the DMV accept this certificate?

Yes. Our 8-Hour Driver's Manual Course is licensed by the Virginia DMV. When you complete the course, you receive an official completion certificate that allows you to retake your learner's permit test as many times as needed.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. The price is $149.95 and that's it. There are no additional charges for your certificate, quiz retakes, "expedited processing," or anything else. Many cheaper courses advertise low prices but add fees at checkout or after you've already started. We don't do that.

I'm under 18 — can I still enroll?

Yes, but you'll need to have completed a 30-hour Driver's Education course first. Email us proof of completion (DEC-1 or DTS 36 form) before enrolling. If you're 18 or older, you can enroll immediately with no prerequisites.

What if I fail a quiz?

You can retake quizzes for free — as many times as you need. There's no additional charge. The quizzes cover material from the course, so if you've been following along, you'll be well prepared.

How long does the course actually take?

The course requires a minimum of 8 hours as required by the Virginia DMV. You can spread this across multiple sessions — most students complete it in 2-3 sittings over a couple of days. You can log out and resume right where you left off.

What happens after I get my certificate?

Print your certificate and bring it to any Virginia DMV location. With your certificate, you can retake the learner's permit test as many times as needed until you pass. There are no additional fees from us.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. A full refund is available before your completion certificate is issued. If you start the course and decide it's not for you, just contact us and we'll process your refund. No questions asked.

What devices can I use?

Any device with a web browser — computer, laptop, tablet, or phone. No special software or app downloads required. Your progress is saved automatically, so you can switch between devices if needed.
