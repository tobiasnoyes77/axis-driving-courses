Online Driver Education Course (DTS)

Virginia DMV-approved driver education for teen drivers. Complete your required coursework online at your own pace, then finish the in-car component with a parent or guardian.

Eligibility

Who Is This Course For?

  • Students who are at least 15 years and 3 months old
  • First-time driver's license applicants under 18
  • Students with a valid Virginia learner's permit
  • Students who have not previously completed a driver education course
Curriculum

What You'll Learn

This course covers everything required by Virginia DMV to complete your driver education program, including traffic laws, defensive driving, and vehicle safety.

Virginia traffic laws and road signs
Defensive driving skills
Safe following distances
Intersections and right-of-way rules
Adverse weather driving
Sharing the road with other users
Driver responsibilities and penalties
Alcohol and drug effects on driving
Vehicle safety checks
Process

How It Works

1
Register online and create your account Sign up and get immediate access to your online coursework.
2
Complete the online coursework at your own pace Work through the required material on any device, anytime. Your progress is saved automatically.
3
Complete the in-car component Finish the required driving hours with a parent or guardian as required by Virginia DMV.
4
Receive your completion certificate Once all requirements are met, your certificate is issued and you're ready for the next step toward your license.
Teen Drivers: A parent or guardian must also complete a required Parent/Teen session as part of this program. Contact us for details.
