Course Eligibility

Who Should Take Our Courses?

Axis Driving Courses offers courses designed for a variety of drivers. Find the group that fits your situation below.

Teen Drivers Under 18

Students under 18 preparing to apply for a Virginia driver's license must complete required driver education programs. These courses help teens learn traffic laws, defensive driving techniques, and safe driving habits.

Parents or Guardians of Teen Drivers

Parents and guardians may also participate in required driver education components, including the Parent/Teen driving safety session, which helps families understand licensing requirements and safe driving expectations.

First-Time Drivers

If you have never held a driver's license before, our courses provide the knowledge needed to prepare for the DMV permit test and begin learning safe driving skills.

Drivers Improving Their Record

Drivers may also take courses to improve driving knowledge, satisfy court requirements, or earn safe driving points.
